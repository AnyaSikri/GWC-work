Ron Weasley

Ron's real name is Ronald Billius Weasley, but he prefers to be called Ron. He has 6 more siblings along with his mother and father. He is the younger brother of Bill, Charlie, Percy, Fred,George, and the older brother of Ginny. He as well as Harry was sorted into Gryfindoor(A house in Hogwarts). Ron is very scared of spiders, but later in the books he has to go to a place full of spiders and face his fear. Ron is very close to his family, though sometimes he is ashamed becuase of their lack of money. He usually gets his older brothers' items and gets annoyed. Ron was funny, but at times he was also immature and emotionally insensitive. One time Hermione commented that he had the , " Emotional range of a teaspoon."

Hermione Granger

Hermione Granger, personally is my FAVORITE CHARACATER. She is the smartest witch in Hogwarts and extremely clever. She is a Muggle-born, meaning that she was born to two non-wizard parents. She is always at the top of the class because she is extremely hard-working and intelligent. Because of this though, many people tease her for being a bookworm and a muggle-born. She was responsible, organized, and a perfectionist which later led to her being a prefect. Despite her confidence in her abilities, she was never arrogant and she helped her classmates whenever she could. Hermione was extremely loyal to her friends and risked her life many times for them.
